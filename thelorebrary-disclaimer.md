# The Lorebrary - Disclaimer

Welcome to **The Lorebrary**. This project was born from a personal need as an avid fantasy and sci-fi reader of very long series. Often, we have to wait years for a new installment, or life simply gets in the way of finishing a 2,000-page epic. It is easy to forget the intricate characters, details, and plot lines that make these stories so beloved. The Lorebrary is designed to help readers "panic catchup" and bridge those gaps.

My goal is to encourage reading, not replace it. This project is intended to support authors and is in no way meant to undermine their works. These novels are popular for a reason; writers pour their souls into these stories. As such, these summaries are intentionally brief and "soulless"—focusing strictly on plot points rather than prose or thematic depth—because they are **not** meant to replace actual reading. They are tools to reactivate the journeys we have already taken. 

Authors are the ones who write the soul into these works. Please support them by purchasing their books, and consider supporting your local bookstores and libraries in the process.

*Every book page on this site includes direct purchase links to Bookshop.org and Barnes & Noble, as well as a reference to library access via Libby. These links are provided in good faith as part of this project's commitment to supporting authors and the broader book ecosystem.*
