### Chapter 36

Nesta wears the Mask and uses its ancient power to summon a legion of dead warriors from the bottom of the Bog of Oorid. The kelpie is paralyzed by fear before the dead surge upward and rip the creature to shreds at Nesta's command. While the Mask whispers of power to her, Nesta finds herself able to control the thousands of bodies resting beneath the water. She feels no pain or need for air while submerged, witnessing the dead rise in marching formation to slaughter her attacker. Nesta looks upon the dying kelpie without mercy, smiling as the creature is destroyed by the skeletal army.

Cassian and Azriel search frantically for Nesta in the black water, unable to reach Rhysand through their mental bond. They witness the surface ripple and a forest of ancient spears break the water as the undead legion emerges. The dead warriors, ranging from bare skeletons to decaying lords in fine armor, kneel in the shallows as Nesta rises on a pillar of water. She holds the severed head of the kelpie, her eyes burning with silver fire behind the golden Mask. Cassian and Azriel bow before her, recognizing the primal power of Death she now commands.

Nesta removes the Mask, causing the undead army to collapse and vanish back into the murky depths of the bog. She falls into the water as the power leaves her, and Cassian lunges forward to catch her limp body. He hauls her to the shore where she clutches the Mask to her chest and shakes violently from the experience. Azriel keeps his sword drawn to guard against further threats while Cassian holds Nesta close until she loses consciousness. They remain on the shore of Oorid until the mercy of oblivion takes her.



### Chapter 37

The inner circle gathers in a circular room within the Court of Nightmares to interrogate two captured Autumn Court soldiers. These males are chained above a pit of hibernating beasts that feed on the blood trickling through the floor grates. Rhysand and Amren observe that the soldiers are under an enchantment that strips them of sanity and leaves them with only a drive to kill. Azriel has already begun a session of torture, but the males remain vacant and unresponsive to pain. Feyre questions the morality of harming them if they are being magically controlled by Briallyn or Koschei.

Rhysand agrees to stop the torture and decides to contact Helion to break the spells on the captives' minds. The group moves to a palace room where Nesta has somehow bypassed impenetrable wards to sit with the cloth-covered Mask. Nesta explains that she found herself beckoned to the room and that the Mask seemed to open the door for her. Amren warns that most who wear the Mask can never remove it, but Rhysand notes that only someone Made from the same source can rule it. Nesta expresses a desire to destroy the Mask, but Amren explains that it can only be contained.

Nesta moves to a guest suite in the Hewn City and bathes in a sunken pool to wash away the bog's filth. She reflects on the seductive void of the Mask's power and how it briefly erased all her guilt and hatred. Cassian arrives with food and finds her standing naked in the bath, her body covered in healing scratches. They engage in a sexual encounter on the bed, where Cassian eventually takes her from behind with intense ferocity. After they both reach a violent climax, Cassian abruptly dresses and leaves the room with a wicked smile, leaving Nesta alone.



### Chapter 38

During morning training, Emerie and Gwyn tease Nesta about the sultry looks she and Cassian have been exchanging. Nesta attempts to maintain a neutral expression while performing abdominal curls, though her cheeks flush at their persistent questioning. Cassian barks orders from across the ring to keep the priestesses focused on their exercises instead of gossiping. Gwyn suggests a Valkyrie technique called Mind-Stilling to help Nesta steady her emotions and concentrate. This practice involves deep breathing and letting the mind go quiet to remain calm in the face of fear or battle.

Nesta requests a copy of the chapter on Mind-Stilling and swears her friends to secrecy so Cassian will be surprised by her new composure. When the lesson ends, Cassian approaches the trio and senses they have been discussing him. Nesta challenges him on when they will begin training with actual weapons like swords or bows. Cassian leads them to a rack of wooden practice swords, insisting they must treat weapons of death with respect. He notes that he practiced for seven years before he was allowed to touch a real steel blade.

Nesta takes up a double-handed grip on the practice sword and feels the weight of the replica Illyrian blade. Cassian walks her through eight different cuts and blocks, emphasizing the need to lead with the hilt and use her entire body. As Nesta practices the movements, she visualizes past enemies and failures, fueling her resolve to never be weak again. The movements begin to flow faster and surer as she channels her internal rage into the wooden sword. The other priestesses watch in silence as Nesta and Cassian share a moment of intense focus during the drill.



### Chapter 39

Nesta finishes her work in the library and spends the late afternoon running up and down the outside stairwell of the House of Wind. She repeats the mantra "never again" with every step, driven by the memory of her terror in the bog. The following day, eleven priestesses gather in the cold rain to continue their combat training without using magic for shelter. Gwyn arrives wearing Nesta's old Illyrian leathers, which carry the scent of past training sessions. Cassian decides the growing group needs another tutor and enlists Azriel to assist with the morning lessons.

Azriel remains aloof and quiet during the sessions, allowing the priestesses to feel comfortable despite his presence as a second male. Cassian shows the women a technique involving a pressure point on the neck that can knock an opponent unconscious instantly. Gwyn appears distracted and watches Azriel, who was the one who rescued her from the temple at Sangravah. During the lesson, Cassian briefly touches Nesta's neck to position her fingers, and a spark of tension passes between them. After an hour of sword practice, the group concludes the physical portion of their training for the day.

Nesta and Gwyn retreat to a quiet nook in the library to practice the Mind-Stilling technique they found in the Valkyrie texts. Gwyn leads the session, instructing Nesta to focus on her breathing and acknowledge surrounding sounds before letting them fade away. Nesta struggles with intrusive thoughts about her sisters, Elain and Feyre, but eventually manages to let them pass like surf over a rock. For a few moments, her mind stops its constant wandering and sits in a state of contented rest. When she opens her eyes, Nesta feels a rare sense of being settled within her own skin.



### Chapter 40

Cassian takes Nesta to a blacksmith's shop on the outskirts of Velaris to show her how a blade is forged. The High Fae blacksmith initially hesitates but allows them entry after Nesta speaks with a surprising hint of deference. He explains the stages of forging, and Nesta eventually asks to try hammering a practice blade herself. After a clumsy start, she develops a rhythmic swing and successfully hammers out a dagger and then a great sword. Cassian watches with pride as Nesta unleashes the same intensity she brings to her combat training sessions.

The following day, Nesta continues her training in the icy rain and spends the final minutes of the lesson punching a padded tree trunk. She channels three days of pent-up fire and rage into the wood, her knuckles singing with each impact. Azriel and the other priestesses have already departed, leaving only Cassian to observe her relentless assault on the punching block. Nesta does not feel the cold or the wet as she throws jab after jab with sharp, blade-like breaths. She eventually delivers a final, shattering blow that causes the heavy wood beam to splinter.

Lucien arrives at the training ring to observe Nesta and expresses shock at the ferocity of her training progress. He murmurs that some things should perhaps not be awoken while watching her deliver the powerful knockout blow. Nesta ignores both males and stalks back into the House of Wind with a flicker of silver fire in her eyes. After she leaves, Cassian inspects the punching block and finds a glowing burn mark where her fist made contact. The entire block begins to smolder from within and eventually dissolves into a pile of cold cinders.
