### Chapter 56

Winter arrives in Velaris, and the Valkyries continue their training in the frosted ring. Nesta undergoes physical changes as her body strengthens and her health improves through exercise and her relationship with Cassian. She spends two sessions a week practicing various courtly dances with Mor using a Veritas orb, a magical glass sphere that projects memories of music and movement. Mor advises Nesta on selecting a dress for the upcoming Winter Solstice ball to impress Eris. Nesta begins to build a tentative friendship with Mor during these lessons.

Nesta visits the library and encounters Emerie, who is visiting the stacks for the first time. Gwyn presents Nesta and Emerie with a manuscript from Merrill that details the rebirth of the Valkyries. The inclusion in a historical text provides written recognition of the group's efforts and growth. Later that evening, Nesta intentionally sits by a fire in her room to confront the trauma associated with her father's death. She successfully uses breathing techniques to remain in the room until the flames die out.

The House of Wind leaves a trail of evergreen twigs to guide Nesta deep into the library's lowest levels. She follows the path into the absolute darkness of the pit where the House reveals its "heart" to her. Nesta observes that the sentient building shares a similar brokenness and darkness with the priestesses and herself. She embraces the gloom and thanks the House for its companionship and trust. This interaction serves as a Winter Solstice gift from the sentient building to its inhabitant.



### Chapter 57

The Inner Circle travels to the Court of Nightmares for the Winter Solstice celebration. Feyre publicly reveals her pregnancy by wearing a dress that accentuates her swelling belly. Rhysand exerts his dominance as High Lord while the mountain rumbles with his dark power. Keir and Eris congratulate the royal couple, though their sincerity is questioned by the Night Court members. Rhysand presents Eris with a Made dagger, a magically imbued weapon that operates outside normal rules, as a strategic gift.

Nesta enters the throne room wearing a skintight black velvet gown and a crown of black stone spikes. She commands the room's attention and begins a complex waltz with Eris on the dance floor. Her movements are perfectly synchronized with the music, showcasing her exceptional skill and athleticism. She performs twelve solo spins, a feat of daring that leaves the spectators in shocked silence. Eris watches her with feral delight as she completes the traditional dance requirements.

Cassian interrupts the second dance to claim a waltz with Nesta, causing a tense confrontation with Eris. He leads Nesta through the music with determination, and they move in synchronization amidst the hostile crowd. Afterward, Eris approaches Rhysand and offers to provide his armies in exchange for Nesta's hand in marriage. Rhysand tells Eris he will consider the proposal but insists that the decision belongs to Nesta. Cassian monitors the conversation through a mental link with Rhysand and suppresses his physical reactions.



### Chapter 58

The group gathers at the river house to celebrate Winter Solstice and Feyre’s birthday. Nesta and Elain share a rare moment of laughter after a brief verbal sparring match. Lucien attends the party but remains isolated as Elain continues to avoid direct interaction with him. Mor expresses interest in joining the Valkyrie training sessions once her diplomatic duties in Vallahan are complete. Azriel gives Nesta a silver book light, a wand with a glass sphere that emits faelight for reading at night.

Cassian presents Nesta with a Symphonia, a rare device from Helion's court that captures and plays back music. Nesta initially rejects the gift and claims she will marry Eris to punish herself for her perceived unworthiness. This sparks a volatile argument where Cassian confesses his jealousy and Nesta admits her fear of losing him. She reveals that she has pushed him away because she believes she does not deserve his goodness. They eventually reconcile as Nesta acknowledges her love and the power Cassian holds over her.

The couple moves to the bedroom where they engage in an emotional sexual encounter. Nesta experiences a breakdown of her inner mental walls as they move in perfect physical synchronization. They exchange vows of mutual belonging, which are accompanied by the appearance of glowing golden soul-threads. The act concludes with their souls bonding through these visible manifestations of light. Nesta finds sleep in Cassian’s arms after the intensity of their shared connection.



### Chapter 59

Nesta wakes up in Cassian's arms and enjoys a morning of quiet intimacy before he departs. Cassian leaves abruptly to participate in an annual snowball fight with Rhysand and Azriel at their mountain cabin. He informs Nesta that he must also conduct a multi-day inspection of the Illyrian legions. Three days pass without word from Cassian, leaving Nesta feeling restless and experiencing physical desire for his return. Azriel takes over the training sessions and maintains a professional, aloof demeanor while the Valkyries practice sprints.

Nesta invites Emerie and Gwyn to stay overnight at the House of Wind for a social gathering. The sentient House provides magical treats and surreal entertainments, such as bubble-filled bathtubs and a miniature pegasus, a small winged horse that grazes on illusory grass. Gwyn shares the story of her twin sister, Catrin, and the deep bond they shared before Catrin’s death. The conversation shifts toward their shared trauma and the difficulty of reintegrating into the world outside the library. The three women spend the night reading in silence while enjoying the House's hospitality.

The three women decide to make friendship bracelets using various colored threads and silver charms. Gwyn teaches the others how to knot the thread while they engage in idle, supportive chatter. Nesta takes the three charms and makes a collective wish for the group to find courage in the future. She wishes that they will always find their way back to each other regardless of where their lives lead. The silver coins glow faintly in her palm, indicating the magic of the House has acknowledged her request.



### Chapter 60

Cassian returns after five days to find the three Valkyries practicing in the training ring. Gwyn successfully cuts the ribbon with a perfect backhanded slice, fulfilling a long-standing goal of the group. Emerie and Nesta follow suit, and each woman is crowned with a severed piece of the white fabric. This achievement marks their official transition into the ranks of the Valkyries. Cassian and Azriel witness the event and observe the newfound confidence and unity among the three friends.

The trainers introduce a complex obstacle course designed to test the females' ability to work as a cohesive unit. The course changes every night, forcing the participants to adapt to new mental and physical challenges. For two months, the Valkyries endure military drills, phalanxes, and endurance exercises to forge themselves into warriors. They study ancient lore and military strategy during their evenings in the library to supplement their physical training. The other priestesses in the library begin to join the movement and successfully cut the ribbon themselves.

Nesta, Gwyn, and Emerie finally complete the obstacle course under the watchful eyes of Illyrian leaders. Cassian reveals that the course was actually a series of routes from previous Illyrian Blood Rite Qualifiers, the brutal trial for Illyrian warriors. Lord Devlon watches from the pit's edge, visibly enraged by the skill displayed by the female warriors. The trainers explain that the trio has now proven they are as capable as any trained Illyrian unit. Despite the comparison to Illyrian soldiers, the women reaffirm their identity as Valkyries and celebrate their hard-won victory.
