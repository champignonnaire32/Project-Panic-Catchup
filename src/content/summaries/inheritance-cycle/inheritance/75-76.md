### Chapter 75: Promises, New and Old

Eragon and Saphira travel to an estate where Blédhgarm and the elves prepare the Eldunarí for transport to Du Weldenvarden. The Rider gathers the Eldunarí of Glaedr and Umaroth to accompany him on a final journey to Vroengard. Saphira flies Eragon northwest across the grasslands and they camp by a rivulet under the stars. They reach an Urgal village near Lake Flam to meet with Nar Garzhvog and the Herndall council. Eragon participates in a feast where he drinks strong Urgal wine and observes the Kull children.

In the morning, Eragon meets the twelve dams of the Herndall in a smoke-filled circular hut. He proposes a plan for the Urgals to hold regular games of strength and agility open to all races. The Herndall agree to the games after Eragon confirms that Nasuada, Orik, and Arya support the idea. Eragon then suggests a second, more significant plan that causes the council to fall silent in contemplation. The dams accept his proposal and declare Eragon and Saphira the greatest friends the Urgals have ever had.

On Vroengard, Eragon uses the Name of Names to give proper titles to shadow birds and burrow grubs. He and Saphira descend into the Rock of Kuthian to retrieve the hidden dragon eggs and the remaining Eldunarí. Cuaroc, the metal-bodied guardian, joins them as they transport the eggs in a magical pocket of space. They fly over the Spine and Palancar Valley before reaching Ellesméra to meet Arya, Roran, and Katrina. Eragon uses the Name of Names to forge a new compact, allowing dwarves and Urgals to become Dragon Riders.



### Chapter 76: Leave-Taking

Eragon and Saphira spend a final week in Ellesméra visiting the Crags of Tel’naeír and the city gardens. Blédhgarm sends word that the Eldunarí have reached Ardwen Lake, signaling it is time for the final departure. Eragon says goodbye to Katrina and baby Ismira at dawn before flying eastward with Roran and Cuaroc. They reach the city of Sílthrim and find the white ship Talíta waiting at the wharves. The elves load the Eldunarí, food, and supplies onto the vessel for the journey out of Alagaésia.

Eragon gives two dragon eggs to the elves to be delivered to the dwarves and Urgals. Three Eldunarí choose to remain in Alagaésia to mentor Firnen and the future hatchlings. The group boards the Talíta and sails down the Gaena River through the dense elven forest. Saphira and Firnen fly overhead or rest together on the riverbanks during the slow journey. The ship passes through Eldor Lake during a storm and continues south along the Edda River to Hedarth.

King Orik meets the group at Hedarth with hundreds of dwarves and a massive feast of roasted Nagran. Orik presents Saphira with a magical gold and silver ring for her claw that silences her movements. Eragon gives Roran a casket of gold and gems to fund the rebuilding of Carvahall. Arya and Eragon exchange their true names on the deck of the ship as they reach the first curve. Firnen carries Arya away from the vessel, leaving Eragon and Saphira to sail into the unknown eastern lands.
