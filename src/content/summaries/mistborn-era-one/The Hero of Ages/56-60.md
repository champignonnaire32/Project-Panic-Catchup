### Chapter 56

Spook stood in a dark cavern examining his plans to trick Quellion into using Allomancy publicly. Beldre entered Spook's alcove wearing a green and white dress. She looked at Spook's charcoal board, which he had written in Eastern street slang. Spook told Beldre that he intended to threaten Quellion to make him use his powers. Beldre offered to write a letter to Quellion instead, and Spook agreed to read it before sending it.

Sazed, Breeze, and Goradel watched from a window as Quellion's troops abandoned their camp outside the Ministry building. Goradel sent scouts to track the retreating army. Sazed joined a group of soldiers constructing wooden counterweights to block the water flowing into the cavern. Sazed finished checking the floodgate pulleys and returned to his engineering calculations. Spook approached Sazed and reported that the soldiers had left to put out a fire in the city.

Spook and Sazed walked to the edge of the underground lake. Sazed asked Spook if he ever worried that their plans were doomed to fail. Spook admitted his doubts but maintained his optimism about their mission. Spook stated that faith means trusting that someone will eventually make things right. Sazed listened to Spook's words and resolved to regain his own lost faith.



### Chapter 57

Vin sat in her silver-reinforced cell listening to Ruin command her to kill the guards outside. She reached out with her Allomancy and sensed the thousand koloss that Elend had previously given her. Ruin appeared in the cell wearing the physical form of Vin's deceased brother, Reen. Ruin taunted Vin for failing to kill Yomen or escape her imprisonment. Vin realized that Ruin was gloating about his impending victory over the world.

An earthquake shook the prison walls while Ruin smiled at the destruction. Vin recognized human emotions in Ruin and decided he could be manipulated. She asked Ruin what he had done to his opposing force, Preservation. Ruin explained that Preservation had sacrificed himself to create a prison for Ruin thousands of years ago. Ruin revealed that he had finally escaped this prison when Vin gave up the power at the Well of Ascension.

Ruin stated that his original bargain with Preservation allowed him to eventually destroy all life. Ruin claimed that Preservation was now completely dead and unable to stop the apocalypse. Ruin informed Vin that the end of the world would occur in a matter of days. Vin deduced that Ruin only visited her because he believed she could no longer stop him. Vin determined that she had to escape her cell immediately to save mankind.



### Chapter 58

Sazed instructed Goradel's soldiers on how to release the counterweights and flood the city's canals. Spook ordered his men to throw the lever at nightfall if they did not receive a messenger. Beldre begged Spook to cancel the attack, but he refused and placed her under guard. Spook walked to the city square to attend a public speech given by Quellion. A beggar approached Spook and reported that unexpected riots and fires had broken out across the city.

Goradel's soldiers attacked the stage, causing Quellion's guards to rush into combat. Spook leaped onto the stage, drew a dueling cane, and attacked Quellion. A Thug intercepted Spook, forcing Spook to shatter his cane against the man's head and take his obsidian-lined cudgel. Spook grabbed Quellion and raised the weapon to strike him down. Beldre shoved her way to the front of the crowd and shot a coin into Spook's shoulder.

The crowd realized Beldre was an Allomancer and pushed her onto the stage. The vision of Kelsier ordered Spook to murder Beldre with a spike to gain her powers. Spook pulled a bronze spike out of Quellion's arm, causing Kelsier to scream in rage. Spook sliced his own chest with a glass dagger and removed a piece of embedded steel. Kelsier instantly vanished, and Spook charged through the burning Ministry building to manually pull the flood lever.



### Chapter 59

TenSoon walked through the burned ruins of Urteau in his canine body. He observed that a third of the city had burned down, but the canals were now filled with water. TenSoon followed the flow of people to a Ministry building guarded by Elend's soldiers. He walked up the steps and shocked the guards by speaking to them in a human voice. TenSoon claimed he brought a message from Emperor Venture and was allowed inside the building.

TenSoon found Breeze sitting in a large chair and drinking wine while listening to citizen petitions. Breeze recognized TenSoon as the kandra who had previously acted as Vin's wolfhound. TenSoon informed Breeze that he was searching for Vin to deliver news about the mists and the ash. Breeze directed TenSoon to speak with Sazed in a smaller Ministry office. Sazed explained that Vin and Elend had traveled across the empire to Fadrex City.

TenSoon realized that reaching Fadrex City would take him several weeks of running. Sazed asked TenSoon what specific business he had with Vin. TenSoon hesitated because Keepers like Sazed were known for prying into kandra secrets. TenSoon vaguely stated that his news concerned the Hero of Ages and the end of the world. Sazed ignored the religious implications of TenSoon's words and offered him travel provisions.



### Chapter 60

Vin attacked her guards with a wooden leg from her cot when they opened her cell door. She struck down four of the hazekillers before surrendering to the remaining twenty soldiers. The soldiers manacled Vin's arms and marched her into Yomen's audience chamber. Vin rubbed her hands with grease from her food and slipped free of the manacles. She threw silver screws at the guards and charged at Yomen, but he dodged her attacks by burning atium.

Vin returned to the bench and sat down next to the invisible manifestation of Ruin. Yomen informed Vin that it was time for her execution trial and asked why she had invaded his city. Vin explained that her army had come to seize the Lord Ruler's local supply cache. Yomen stated that he still worshipped the Lord Ruler and did not believe Vin had killed him. Vin recounted the exact events of the Lord Ruler's death, but Yomen refused to accept her story.

Yomen theorized that the Lord Ruler had faked his death so the skaa would worship Vin instead. Vin realized that Yomen was keeping her alive to find proof that the Lord Ruler was still active. Yomen demanded that Vin order Elend's army to retreat and leave the cache's food supply alone. Yomen revealed that he considered the massive stockpile of atium in the city to be completely worthless. The guards pulled Vin to her feet and dragged her back toward her prison cell.
