### Chapter 71

Sazed sits with the First Generation of kandra and discusses the prophecies regarding the Hero of Ages. The kandra confirm that Ruin and Preservation created the world together, with a pact allowing Ruin to eventually destroy it. Preservation sacrificed his mind to imprison Ruin and protect humanity. Haddek reveals that the power Vin released at the Well of Ascension was Ruin's mind, while his physical power is hidden elsewhere. The kandra explain that Ruin is now searching for this physical body.

Sazed walks to the center of the metal dais in the kandra chamber. He taps his pewtermind to gain physical strength and pushes aside the heavy metal disk covering a pit. Inside the pit, Sazed discovers the Lord Ruler's massive stockpile of atium beads. Haddek explains that the kandra gathered this atium over centuries as payment for their Contracts. Sazed realizes that the atium itself is the concentrated physical body of Ruin's power.

Elsewhere, TenSoon travels toward Fadrex City in the body of a massive horse. He struggles through extremely deep ash that falls heavily across the landscape. Upon cresting a hill, TenSoon stops and stares at the burning plains before him. He sees that the Tyrian ashmount has erupted violently and covered the surrounding area in flowing lava. Despairing over the destruction, TenSoon realizes he is moving too slowly and considers abandoning his journey.



### Chapter 72

Vin arrives in Luthadel and lands on the city wall amidst a cold, continuous rain. She looks out over the ruined, empty city and senses multiple Allomancers homing in on her position. She leaps toward Kredik Shaw, intending to spring a trap against Ruin. Ruin speaks to her, realizing that she does not have the atium and threatening to destroy her. Lightning flashes, revealing thirteen Inquisitors surrounding Vin in the rain, including Marsh.

Vin immediately launches into battle, burning duralumin and Pushing herself among the Inquisitors. She rips an eye-spike out of one Inquisitor, but the creature survives and continues fighting. The Inquisitors utilize Feruchemical speed and strength, indicating they have been spiked with new abilities. Despite her desperate efforts, Vin quickly runs out of pewter and suffers a broken shoulder. She attempts to draw upon the mists for power, but the mists physically retreat from her.

Marsh approaches the severely wounded Vin and begins systematically breaking her bones. He shatters both of her legs and snaps her arms while she screams in agony. Deep within his mind, Marsh temporarily overpowers Ruin's control by focusing on his past failures. Using this split second of freedom, Marsh reaches down and rips Vin's bronze earring out of her ear. The mists immediately surge into Vin's body, instantly healing her wounds and granting her immense power.



### Chapter 73

TenSoon stands up in his horse body and notices a sudden change in the environment. He observes that the mists have stopped swirling chaotically and are now flowing in a single direction. The mists course around him like a massive river heading straight toward Luthadel. Feeling a renewed sense of hope, TenSoon turns around and gallops back the way he came. In Fadrex City, Breeze and Allrianne also spot the mists accelerating outside their window.

Spook bursts into Breeze's room and urgently orders them to move everyone into the storage caverns. Meanwhile, Elend rests against a rock outcropping while his soldiers fight a losing battle against the koloss. A messenger approaches Elend and reports that the koloss are suddenly retreating from the city gates. Elend Pushes himself into the sky and sees the koloss army running rapidly toward Luthadel. He also notices that the mists continue to pull eastward, drawn by the same invisible force.

Empowered by the mists, Vin easily fights the Inquisitors with unlimited Allomantic reserves. She kills multiple Inquisitors by ripping out their spikes and using them as projectiles against the others. A massive Push launches Vin high into the sky, where she draws the surrounding mists into a giant vortex. She plummets back to the ground and unleashes a metallic shockwave that completely flattens Kredik Shaw. After executing the remaining Inquisitors, Vin reaches for Marsh's spikes, but her physical body suddenly vanishes into mist.



### Chapter 74

Elend stands atop the rocky heights outside Fadrex City and observes the aftermath of the koloss battle. He notes that the mists have completely vanished with the sunrise, causing the terrified skaa to hide indoors. The sun feels unnaturally hot against Elend's skin, and heavy ash continues to bury the surrounding corpses. Elend orders Yomen to form search crews to look for wounded survivors among the dead. He also commands Yomen to gather the remaining population and move them into the underground storage caverns.

Elend turns eastward and informs Yomen that he intends to leave the city to search for Vin. He realizes that he cannot help Fadrex any further and must go find his wife. Yomen approaches Elend and formally apologizes for his previous actions and harsh judgments. Yomen asserts his belief that the Lord Ruler is still guiding events through both believers and non-believers. He then reaches up to his forehead and removes a single bead of atium from his brow.

Yomen hands his last bead of atium to Elend for use in the upcoming conflicts. Elend examines the bead, noting that he has never personally burned atium before. Yomen reveals that he is actually an atium Misting, explaining how he previously mimicked Mistborn abilities. Yomen instructs Elend to take the atium bead and go find his wife. Elend accepts the metal, tucks it away, and departs Fadrex City using his horseshoe-jumping technique.



### Chapter 75

Sazed sits in his kandra cavern and reviews his metallic notes regarding the Terris religion. He reads the historical journals of the believers and analyzes their accounts of miracles. After considering his own recent experiences, Sazed makes a personal decision to adopt the Terris faith. He stands up and gathers his metal sheets to prepare for another interview. Sazed walks through the cavern corridors and enters the Trustwarren to speak with the First Generation.

Inside the Trustwarren, Sazed finds the Second Generation attempting to calm a crowd of anxious kandra. The younger kandra are panicking because the mists have entirely disappeared from the outside world. Haddek orders the room cleared, leaving only Sazed and the First Generation inside the chamber. The elders reveal that the Lord Ruler commanded them to remove their Blessings and commit mass suicide when the mists vanished. Sazed deduces that someone else has absorbed Preservation's power to become the Hero of Ages.

Before Sazed can explain his theory, KanPaar and a group of armed kandra burst into the Trustwarren. KanPaar declares a coup and refuses to let the First Generation execute their mass suicide. Fifth Generation kandra quickly disarm Sazed and forcefully take away his pewtermind. KanPaar orders his soldiers to bind and gag the members of the First Generation. Sazed is dragged out of the Trustwarren, leaving the massive atium stockpile in the hands of the Second Generation.
