### Chapter 61

Elend returns to his camp outside Fadrex City and greets Ham. He commands a massive army of twenty-eight thousand new koloss, bringing his total forces to thirty-seven thousand. Ham informs Elend that the surrounding empire is falling to ash, earthquakes, and lava. Many conquered cities are starving or abandoned, leaving a trail of corpses. Despite the grim news, Elend decides they must not give up.

Elend instructs Ham to have the soldiers post scouts to watch the remaining koloss. He reveals that Vin is bonded to some of the koloss and that they would go wild if she were dead. Because the koloss remain under control, Elend knows Vin is still alive inside the city. Ham mentions that they sent a messenger to Yomen under Elend's name to confirm Vin's status. Elend ignores an apparent warning from the mist spirit and decides he cannot afford a lengthy siege.

Elend resolves to attack Fadrex City to rescue Vin and secure the storage cavern. He speaks to Cett and confirms the plan to launch a surprise attack the following morning. The koloss will lead the charge to break the enemy defenses. The human soldiers will follow behind to secure the city. Elend changes his clothes and mentally prepares himself for the difficult decisions ahead.



### Chapter 62

Sazed visits Spook's sickroom and observes the young man's severe burn injuries. He notes that Spook's slow healing proves he is not secretly a Pewterarm. Beldre sits beside Spook's bed and expresses her deep hope for his recovery. Sazed returns to his quarters and finishes reviewing his extensive portfolio of religions. He realizes that every single faith contains logical fallacies and fails to explain the Lord Ruler's oppression.

Depressed by his findings, Sazed struggles with the realization that he has found no true religion. He opens the tome he compiled with Tindwyl and finds a picture of a flower given to him by Vin. Sazed weeps over his lack of faith and the pain of feeling betrayed by the universe. Outside his room, he overhears Breeze attempting to persuade TenSoon to stay longer. TenSoon declines and refers to Sazed as the "Announcer."

Sazed bursts into the hallway and demands to know why TenSoon used that specific Terris title. TenSoon reveals that the Lord Ruler transformed the living Feruchemists into mistwraiths but made his trusted packmen into the First Generation of kandra. Sazed realizes that the original Terris religion still survives in the memories of these immortal beings. He quickly packs his bags and decides to travel south with TenSoon to meet the First Generation. Sazed hopes to learn the truth and convince the kandra that the end of the world has arrived.



### Chapter 63

Vin sits in her cell and listens to Ruin speak through the manifestation of Reen. Ruin boasts about the worsening ashfalls and earthquakes tearing the world apart. Vin ignores his taunts and demands the guards take her to speak with Yomen. She enters Yomen's audience chamber and notices his extreme fatigue. Vin tells Yomen that Elend controls the koloss by using a combination of duralumin and emotional Allomancy.

Yomen remains skeptical, but Vin presses on by marking the locations of the five storage caverns on a map. She points out that every cavern is located near a rich metal mine. Vin realizes that the Lord Ruler shielded his maps with metal to hide them from Ruin. She understands that Ruin used her to locate the final cache because he could not find it himself. Vin concludes that Ruin desperately needs the atium hidden inside the Fadrex City cavern to achieve his goals.

The Inquisitor Marsh bursts into the room and demands the atium from Yomen. Yomen confesses that he lied to Vin and that the storage cavern did not actually contain any atium. Ruin loses his temper and temporarily transforms into a swirling black tempest. Marsh orders Yomen to let the city burn before Elend's advancing koloss army. Vin boldly predicts that Elend will not attack because he is a better person than she is.



### Chapter 64

Spook slowly regains consciousness and feels a deep, throbbing pain throughout his body. He opens his eyes and finds Breeze, Beldre, and Goradel standing over his bed. Beldre gently informs Spook that his entire body is encased in casts and bandages. Breeze tells the injured young man that Sazed has already left the city to travel south. Beldre explains that Urteau is mostly safe but the ashfalls are growing worse.

Spook remembers his vision and insists that he must send an urgent message to Vin. He explains that a dark force controlled him and the Citizen. Breeze tries to soothe Spook and tells him that travel is too difficult right now. Beldre urges Spook to rest and promises they will send the message later. Spook grinds his teeth in frustration and refuses to let the matter drop.

Goradel steps forward and volunteers to deliver the vital message to Vin. The soldier explains that he owes Vin his life because she spared him during the rebellion. Goradel swears he will reach her or die trying. Spook smiles at the soldier's determination and accepts the offer. He commands Goradel to bring him a thin sheet of metal so he can write the message in steel.



### Chapter 65

Elend stands on a rock outcropping and watches his koloss army prepare to assault Fadrex City. Ham approaches and questions the morality of using the brutal creatures to slaughter innocents. Elend realizes that Vin would not want him to destroy a city just to save her. He abruptly changes his mind and orders Ham to sound the retreat. Elend commands his human soldiers to break camp and directs his koloss to run away from the city.

Inside the city, Vin realizes that Ruin used the mists and her own emotions to manipulate her. Marsh shakes Vin and violently demands to know the location of the missing atium. Ruin commands Yomen to order his soldiers out of their fortifications to attack Elend's retreating army. Yomen obeys the dark god and sends his men charging into the deep ash. Suddenly, Ruin severs Elend's Allomantic control over the koloss army.

The koloss turn around and brutally attack Elend's retreating human soldiers. Vin consumes a stolen vial of metals and uses duralumin to shoot her earring through Marsh's skull. She commands her own small group of koloss to attack Yomen's advancing soldiers. Vin draws upon the mists to briefly overpower Marsh before he flees into the sky. Vin then escapes through the window and reunites with Elend on the battlefield.
